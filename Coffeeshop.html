OUR PROCESS

Roasted by Hand, Daily

Every morning, our family roasts fresh batches in our small roastery. We carefully monitor each roast, adjusting by feel and experience to bring out the best in every bean.

It's a labor of love that's been passed down through our family. We believe great coffee comes from patience, care, and knowing exactly what we're serving to our guests.

OUR FAMILY STORY

A Passion Shared

What started as a dream in our family kitchen has grown into this cozy café. We source our beans directly from farmers we've built relationships with over the years, often visiting them to understand their craft.

Every member of our family plays a role—from roasting to serving. We're here because we love what we do and the community we've built around great coffee.
