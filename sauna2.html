The Maker

Built with heart.
Crafted with soul.

Sacred Ember was born from a simple belief — that the spaces we inhabit shape how we feel, how we heal, and how we connect. Based in Yorkshire, we design and build bespoke saunas and wellbeing huts for people who take their health seriously.

Every structure is a blend of Scandinavian minimalist metalwork and warm, bespoke natural timber. The floors are as considered as the ceiling. The fire is the heart of everything.

"Creating saunas and wellbeing spaces with heart and soul — for people who care deeply about themselves, their families, and their surroundings."

Our Philosophy

Heart-Centred Wellness

We create saunas and wellbeing spaces with heart and soul. Each structure is a sanctuary designed to honour the sacred ritual of self-care — bringing together the elemental power of fire with the warmth of natural timber and the precision of Scandinavian minimalism.

Elemental Fire

Wood-fired traditions that connect you to ancient wellness practices and the transformative power of flame.

Natural Materials
